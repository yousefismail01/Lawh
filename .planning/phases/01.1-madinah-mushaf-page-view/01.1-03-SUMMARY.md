## Decisions Made
- Lazy page rendering in PagerView: only current +/- 2 pages rendered as MushafPage components, all others are empty placeholder Views -- keeps memory low while PagerView manages 604 children
- Custom View-based slider with PanResponder instead of installing @react-native-community/slider -- reduces dependency surface and allows full RTL control
- Ayah word grouping in MushafLine: consecutive words grouped by surahId+ayahNumber key, each group wrapped in Pressable for long-press detection with subtle highlight
